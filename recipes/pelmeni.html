Pelmeni

Description

Pelmeni, most commonly known as dumplings, is a popular dish most commonly found in post-soviet countries, such as Russia, Ukraine and Khazastan. The inside is filled with various types of meat with thin dough wrapped around it. If you want to steal the heart of an Eastern-European partner, Pelmeni will get you one step closer.

Indgredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Meat of your choice (eg. pork, lamb or beef)

Steps

  1. The first step to making delcious pelmeni is to prepare the dough by rolling it out
  2. Shape the dough into a circle
  3. Add the meat mixture into the center and fold the sides to create a half-moon shape
  4. Boil water and add salt with choice of seasoning
  5. Add pelmeni once water is boiled
  6. Let Pelmeni boil until they float to the top
  7. Once all pelmeni reached the top, wait 5 more minutes before taking them out
  8. As an extra, add sour cream on top of pelmeni
